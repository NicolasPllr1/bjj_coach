Keep your knees between you and your opponent: The essence of bottom position in Jiu jitsu is to use your legs as a barrier between you and your opponent. This is what enables smaller, weaker people to wear down and defeat bigger, stronger people - for the legs of a small person are stronger than the arms of a bigger person. Your first instinct in this game when underneath opponents must always be to get your feet knees and hips between you and the other guy.
