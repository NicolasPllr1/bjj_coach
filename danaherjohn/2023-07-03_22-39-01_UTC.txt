It’s a game of levers and fulcrums - so when it’s time to apply force - APPLY IT AT THE END OF THE LEVER AND BE SURE THAT THE FULCRUM IS IDEALLY POSITIONED. When using levers and fulcrums a difference measured in inches can make a substantial difference in force. Take a little extra time to set the fulcrum and find the end of the lever - you can make a substantial improvement in performance in a minute with this simple insight alone
